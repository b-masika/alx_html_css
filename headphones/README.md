- **Max Content Width:** `1000px` (centered on the page).

- **Display/Logo Font:** Spin-Cycle-OT

### Colors & Interactions
- **Primary Interaction Color:** `#FF6565`
- **Links:** Turn `#FF6565` on hover/active states.
- **Buttons:** Change `opacity` to `0.9` on hover/active states.
